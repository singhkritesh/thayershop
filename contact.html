Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in their original packaging. Apparel must have tags attached.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we only ship within the United States. International shipping options are coming later this year.
How do I know my size for apparel?
We use standard unisex sizing. You can find a detailed size chart on each product page. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for hoodies.
Can I pick up my order on campus?
Yes! Choose "Campus Pickup" during checkout to collect your items from the Demo School administrative office.
